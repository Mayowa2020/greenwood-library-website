Our Story

Founded in 1965, Greenwood Community Library has been a cornerstone of knowledge, education, and culture for our community. Our mission is to provide access to diverse information, resources, and opportunities that enrich lives.

What We Offer

  • Extensive collection of books, e-books, and audiobooks
  • Free internet and computer access
  • Children's reading programs and activities
  • Monthly workshops and events for all ages
